🌶 SpiceSight — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SpiceSight is an AI-powered recipe app. This policy explains, in plain language, what information we handle and why. We built SpiceSight to be simple, and our approach to your data is the same.

What we collect

Account information (only if you sign in). SpiceSight offers optional sign-in with Google. When you sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account. We use these solely to identify your account and display your name in the app. Signing in is optional — the app works without an account.

Your saved recipes. If you sign in, recipes you choose to save are stored in our database (hosted by Supabase) linked to your account so they sync across your devices. If you don't sign in, saved recipes are stored only on your own device and never leave it.

Recipe generation inputs. When you generate a recipe, the ingredients and preferences you enter are sent through our server to Google's Gemini AI service to create your recipe. These inputs are used only for generating your recipe.

Basic technical data. Our server temporarily processes your IP address to enforce fair usage limits (rate limiting). These records are automatically deleted within roughly one hour.

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. We do not show ads. We do not use tracking or analytics services. We do not share your personal information with third parties except the service providers named above (Supabase for storage, Google Gemini for recipe generation), which process data only to provide the app's functionality.

Affiliate links

The shopping list feature may include links to purchase ingredients on Amazon. As an Amazon Associate, SpiceSight earns from qualifying purchases made through these links. Clicking them takes you to Amazon, whose own privacy policy applies there.

Data retention & deleting your account

Your saved recipes remain in your account until you delete them or delete your account. To delete your account and all associated data, email us at the address below with the subject "Delete my account" from the Google email you signed in with, and we will remove your account and all your data within 30 days.

Children

SpiceSight is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and the date above.
