🌶 Delete Your SpiceSight Account

Last updated: July 2026

You can permanently delete your SpiceSight account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this.

Option 1 — Delete directly in the app (fastest)

1
Open SpiceSight and make sure you're signed in.
2
Tap the menu (☰) in the top corner to open your account panel.
3
Tap "Delete my account" at the bottom of the menu.
4
Confirm on the warning dialog. Your account and all saved recipes are permanently removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us at futurespicesight@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account", sent from the Google email address you signed in with. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove: your saved recipes stored in our database, and the association between your account and your Google sign-in. We do not retain a copy. Recipe-generation inputs are never stored long-term, and the temporary technical data used for rate limiting is automatically deleted within about one hour regardless of account status.

What this doesn't affect

Deleting your SpiceSight account does not delete your Google account itself — it only removes your data from SpiceSight. To manage your Google account, visit your Google account settings.
